Our approach

Biomedical researchers are in desperate need of resources that can distill complicated datasets into easy-to-interpret figures/charts

Bioquilt's aim is to provide visually accessible and interpretable data visualizations for age-related diseases

How does it work?

Natural language search for bioinformatics data.
For example: "Is gene_X a viable candidate for immunotherapy in this cancer type?" or
"LRRK2 and Parkinson's disease"

Manually curated datasets for aging-related diseases and age-related cancers (e.g: prostate, colon, etc.) in a visually accessible way with an interactive graphics output
