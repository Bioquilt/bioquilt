Our approach

Biomedical researchers are in desperate need of resources that can distill complicated datasets into easy-to-interpret figures/charts.

Biological datasets are scattered across thousands of databases. These data silos impede integrative data-driven approaches to healthcare.

Our aim is to use AI and machine learning to provide visually accessible and interpretable data visualizations for investigating human health and disease.

What do we work on?

Bring together the latest human omics data from around the world to one platform every 24 hours

Synchronize human health and disease data across thousands of databases and make it visually interpretable to accelerate discovery
