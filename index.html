Our approach

Biomedical researchers are in desperate need of resources that can distill complicated datasets into easy-to-interpret figures/charts

Aging therapeutics and drug target discovery to extend human healthspan is dependent upon data integration and data preparation at scale

Bioquilt's aim is to use AI and machine learning to provide visually accessible and interpretable data visualizations for age-related diseases

How does it work?

Natural language search for bioinformatics data.
For example: "LRRK2 and Parkinson's disease" or
"Is the gene APLNR a viable candidate for immunotherapy in prostate cancer?"

Manually curated datasets for aging-related diseases and age-associated cancers (e.g: prostate, colon, etc.) in a visually accessible way with an interactive graphics output
