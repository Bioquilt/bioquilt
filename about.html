About us:

Bioquilt is an anti-aging biotech company changing the way academic and industry professionals approach the investigation of age-related disease. We specialize in computationally unifying the field of aging from a diverse set of clinical pursuits interspersed across a wide variety of disease areas into a single interdisciplinary and integrative data science. To achieve this transformation, Bioquilt is bringing computing to aging at a time when research in this field is an underdeveloped area and globally unmet need. Our vision is to quilt together the world's aging data across scientific domains ranging from the cardiovascular (coronary artery disease, atherosclerosis, stroke, etc.) to the neurodegenerative (Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, dementia, etc.) to the dermatological (age-associated skin conditions and diseases) to cancer (prostate cancer, colon cancer, multiple myeloma, etc.). Our mission is to fight aging with state-of-the-art computation by providing a common platform for cutting edge discovery.
