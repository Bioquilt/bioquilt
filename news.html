6/19/2018:

Bioquilt debut at GRAIL. Bohdan Khomtchouk, Ph.D. presents invited talk on Bioquilt at GRAIL in Menlo Park, CA. Thank you Joe Rickert, RStudio's "Ambassador at Large" for all things R, for organizing this event as part of the Bay Area R User Group.

6/13/2018:

Nicholas Tsinoremas, Ph.D. joins Bioquilt's Scientific Advisory Board. Bioquilt welcomes Prof. Tsinoremas, Founding Director of the Center for Computational Sciences at the University of Miami. Dr. Tsinoremas is Professor of Medicine, Professor of Health Informatics, and Professor of Computer Science at the University of Miami. Before joining UM, he was Director of Computational Genomics at Merck/Rosetta, Senior Director of Informatics at Scripps Research Institute, and VP Genomics at DoubleTwist Inc.

5/31/2018:

Dario Ghersi, M.D., Ph.D. joins Bioquilt's Scientific Advisory Board. Bioquilt welcomes Dr. Ghersi, Assistant Professor of Biomedical Informatics at the School of Interdisciplinary Informatics at the University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO). His research interests include bioinformatics, computational biology, and genomics. He received his M.D. from the University of Genoa, Italy and his Ph.D. in Computational Biology at New York University. Prior to starting his faculty career at UNO, Dr. Ghersi was a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Mona Singh's lab at Princeton University. His research work involves cancer genomics, computational immunology, structural bioinformatics of protein binding, and protein interaction networks.
